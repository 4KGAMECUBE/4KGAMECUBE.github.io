    • Favourite Stuff

    Panzer Dragoon Saga

    This is the most expensive game I have purchased and it is also my favourite game.
    I really think everyone should play this game, not because it is rare and expensive but because it is so ahead of it's time and unique

    EarthBound

    I wanted to own this for about 9 years though and honestly I have no regrets.
    Love this game and it is one of my most prized possesions.

    Chibi Robo

    Someone was selling this game on ebay for £40 because it wouldn't work.
    I bought it and it played just fine because it was just an NTSC copy and the seller did not know.
    The disc is in perfect condition and it has the manual and box and everything.
    This is one of the best deals I ever got when collecting.

    Virtual Boy Wario Land

    I got this on eBay for about £250 and it was sold as just the console...
    A few weeks later a huge Fedex package arrived with a VB in the box with 2 controllers and 2 games...
    Holy shit.

    Splatoon

    This is the game that got me involved in my first online communities and it means a lot to me because of that.
    It isn't rare or anything but this is definitely one of my favourite games I own.

    Mother series

    I wanted to own all the main games and it was so cool to finally do that. The Mother series is my second favourite gaming franchise, just behind Pikmin

    Mario RPG games

    The Mario rpg games are so much fun, especially the early ones.
    A lot of these games are also very expensive and I am happy to own these. I have the newer ones too but I am not too fond of them.
    Unfortunately my Paper Mario has a fake label but at least it does have a genuine PCB!

    Cave Story

    Fantastic game that I am very happy to own physically.
    4 times... and many more times digitally...

    Futurama

    One of the rarer Xbox games and I even have the rare purple cover.
    Just bought a standard copy from CEX and got sent the rarer variation!

    LR Games

    Some of my favourite indie games. LR games suck but still! They are cool!

    Mario Kart Super Circuit

    The only game I ever bought at a convention. Was a very fun experience :)

    Mario Galaxy

    Childhood favourite. This game is everything to me.
    I got the American copy while visiting Canada. Both copies are very special to me.

    Xenoblade Chronicles

    Two of the best games I have played

    Vib Ribbon

    This game is so cool! You can put any CD into the Playstation and play any song.
    My favourite thing to do is put in a Sega Saturn disc and play the soundtrack of one of those games.

    Katamari Damacy

    This was the reason I bought a PS2.
    Before this game I only really played on Nintendo consoles but this game made me branch out a bit and I am very glad I did.

    Pikmin 2

    Pikmin 2 is a very special game to me.
    I played it during one of the worst moments of my life and I really feel like it gave me something to look forward to doing while going through a lot of trauma.

    Zelda game series

    Full mainline Zelda games. So many great games here

    Mario Kart Wii

    I met so many people through this game. So many friends that I still talk to today! (Hello Gui)

    Everdrives

    The best flash carts available. Some of the best investments I ever made into my collection.

    Wowaka - Unhappy Refrain

    This is my favourite album from my favourite music artist. I feel very privileged to own a copy considering Wowaka's death.

    ABBA Stuff

    Love ABBA. Need more of their music physical. My favourite one is the ABBA Gold VCD

    Panasonic Q + HDMI Modded Gamecube + Tales of Symphonia Gamecube

    Bought this as broken. It just works though after a little bit of wiggling with the drive, the grease must have just hardened over time but now it is completely fine.
    It plays Gamecube games and VCD (I don't own any Japanese DVDS).
    I also have a HDMI modded cube and a rare Tales of Symphonia edition console which I really love :)

    Gamecube Keyboard Controller + Other rare Gamecube controllers.

    I spent way too much money on this, I just had to own one. I also collect other rare Gamecube controllers.
    These are some of my favourites.

    Sound Voltex Controller

    My favourite game controller. I spent the extra money on Sanwa buttons after enjoying SDVX so much. I'm not very good at it but it is still fun!

    Hori N64 and Gamecube controllers

    Hori controllers are always great. These are two of the best.

    Sega Saturn

    I love this console and to show it off to people.
    I like the way games look on this console and the 3D controller is so fun to use. Probably the most interesting video game system to me honestly
